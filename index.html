About us

Espression was built by students seeking their ways on how to express their feelings. Expressing one's feeling is very important not just for us but to everyone and we all seek into ourselves on ways that we could express it and for us we found ourselves expressing it through food which we love, specifically coffee.

That is when Espression coffee was made, we are not only here to express our feelings through our love of coffee but also be able to become an open book for our customers in order for them to express their feelings also by serving them the best organic coffee in town. .

Experience the best quality 100% Arabica coffee beans. Here at Espression, we're not just focused on bringing out the best in our coffee but also bringing out the best of our fellow customers. That's why we will continue to be better so that our customers can be satisfied. For everything is better with a good cup of coffee.
